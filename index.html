SmallTools

Single-purpose tools. Free, instant, private.

Each of these does one job, entirely in your browser. No accounts, no tracking, nothing leaves the page.

Your balance is reported on the statement closing date — not the due date. See exactly how much to pay before the snapshot.

GraceWindow

Your CD's penalty-free exit window.

When a CD matures you get about 7–10 days to move your money before it auto-renews at whatever rate the bank picks. Know your dates.

FeeWindow

That annual fee is refundable — briefly.

Most issuers refund an annual fee if you cancel or downgrade within ~30 days of it posting. See your exact deadline and the smarter move first.
